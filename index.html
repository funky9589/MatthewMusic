About Me

大學主修文學，並於柏克里音樂學院線上學院就讀音樂作曲。這份跨領域的背景讓我能以獨特的視角融合敘事與音樂， 專精於各種風格的影視配樂與古典樂作曲。我曾師從國內外多位作曲家及音樂家，並在管樂團、打擊樂團及作曲比賽中屢獲佳績。 此外，我也積極參與作曲、流行音樂演出及合唱團的作曲與演出。期待能與您合作，將故事化為動人的旋律！

Greetings, all! I hold a university degree in literature and am currently studying music composition at Berklee Online. This interdisciplinary background allows me to uniquely blend narrative and music, specializing in diverse styles of film scoring and classical composition. I've studied with renowned composers and musicians both locally and internationally and have achieved numerous awards in wind bands, percussion ensembles, and composition competitions. Furthermore, I actively engage in songwriting, performing at pop music festivals, and composing and performing for choirs. I look forward to collaborating with you to transform stories into captivating melodies!
